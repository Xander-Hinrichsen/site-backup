Jonathan Zamora

I am an MS in Computer Science student at USC, and I am graduating in May 2025.

I recently completed my undergraduate studies at UC San Diego where I earned a BS in Computer Science in June 2023.

I am advised by Prof. Yue Wang at USC, and have worked under Prof. Xiaolong Wang at UC San Diego. I am interested in deep reinforcement learning, robotics, computer vision, and self-supervised learning to build intelligent agents capable of generalization, long-horizon planning, and continual adaptation in diverse and complex environments.

Outside of research and classes, I enjoy doing leadership and outreach activities which promote Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).
